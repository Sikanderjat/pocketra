# Privacy Policy for Pocketra

**Effective Date:** 26 April 2026

Welcome to Pocketra.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects information when using the application.

---

# 1. Information Collection

Pocketra is designed to provide utility tools such as calculator, weather, flashlight, notes, timer, scanner, and related features.

Depending on the features used, the app may access limited device permissions.

## Information That May Be Accessed

### Location Information

Used only for:

* Weather updates
* Local weather detection

Location data is not permanently stored on servers.

### Camera Access

Used only for:

* QR/Barcode scanning

Camera data is not stored or shared.

### Flashlight Permission

Used only for:

* Torch functionality

No personal information is collected through flashlight usage.

### Storage / Notes Data

If notes or saved preferences are used:

* Data is stored locally on the device
* Not uploaded to external servers unless explicitly stated

# 3. Weather Services

Weather functionality may use third-party weather APIs to fetch forecast information.

Data sent may include:

* City name
